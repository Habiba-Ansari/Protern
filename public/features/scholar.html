Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for our internship scholarships,
please review the eligibility criteria below to ensure you meet the requirements.

Basic Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a current student or recent graduate (within the last 12 months) from an accredited college or university.
  • Must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Must be enrolled in or have completed an internship related to your field of study.
  • Must be a legal resident or citizen of the country where the internship is being offered.

Additional Requirements

  • Submit a completed application form by the specified deadline.
  • Provide a current resume highlighting your educational background and internship experience.
  • Submit a personal statement or essay explaining your career goals and how the scholarship will help you achieve them.
  • Provide two letters of recommendation from professors, employers, or internship supervisors.

Important Notes

Meeting the above criteria does not guarantee selection. Scholarships are awarded based on a combination of academic performance, internship experience, and the quality of your application. We encourage all eligible candidates to apply!
